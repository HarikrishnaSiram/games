How to Play

1. Select a category (Fruits, Animals, or Numbers).

2. Click "Restart" to shuffle the cards.

3. Click on a card to flip it and reveal its value.

4. Find the matching pairs by clicking two cards. If they match, they stay revealed.

5. If they don't match, they will flip back after a short delay.

6. The game ends when all pairs are found. Try to beat your score!
